Summary

As an Interactive Media Designer and Laboratorium Permodelan Digital Assistant at Universitas Sriwijaya (UNSRI), I apply my skills in graphic design, 3D rendering, and Photoshop manipulation to create engaging and immersive digital content for various platforms and purposes. I am also pursuing my Bachelor of Architecture degree at UNSRI, where I learn about architectural engineering, design, and theory, and develop my passion and vision for creating sustainable and innovative spaces and structures. I have participated in several internships and workshops, such as the Architectural Internship at RAW Architecture, where I gained valuable hands-on experience and insights from leading professionals and experts in the field. I am eager to continue expanding my knowledge and skills, and to explore new opportunities and challenges in the intersection of architecture and digital media.
